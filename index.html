Research experience

Most recently I've picked up my own personal research project under the advisement of Yuan Tian, a professor of Computer Science at the University of Virginia. We're working to understand the development and abuse of Adversial Examples to Machine Learning in IoT (Internet of Things) systems.

We test image-recognition software against influence functions to determine the most "weighted" datapoints in large datasets to determine the most impactful characteristics in a an arbitrary datapoint. Using these characteristics, we can have a better understanding of the development of Adversial Examples, datapoints designed intentionally to break image recognition software ..
